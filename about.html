About

Built different, by design.

McCoySites is a solo web design studio. One person, direct accountability, no layers. Small businesses get the same quality as companies ten times their size — without paying for overhead they don't need.

Who We Are

A studio, not an agency.

At agencies, your project passes through a sales team, an account manager, a creative director, and a developer — each one adding time, cost, and distance between you and the final product.

McCoySites cuts all of that. You work directly with the person designing and building your site. That means faster decisions, cleaner output, and a site that actually reflects your business.

We use modern tools — including AI-assisted workflows — to move faster without cutting corners. The result is professional-quality work delivered on a small-business timeline and budget.

Our Process

How a project works

We keep the process tight by design. The more time you spend in back-and-forth, the more your launch date slips.

We come prepared. Every project starts with research into your industry and competitors so we're not starting from scratch when we talk to you.

01
Kickoff Call — 30 min

We cover your business, your customers, your goals, and any existing brand or content. You leave knowing exactly what happens next.

02
Design Review — async

We share a full design mockup for your feedback. You review on your schedule. One clear round of revisions, then we build.

03
Build & QA

We code the site, test across devices and browsers, and hand you a staging link for final review before anything goes live.

04
Launch

We handle deployment, DNS, performance checks, and make sure everything is solid before we call it done.
